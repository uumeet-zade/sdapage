The republic
belongs to all of us.

Social Democratic Alliance is a civic republican party built on accountability, transparency, and the belief that public institutions exist to serve the public — not the other way around.

Our platform

What we stand for

Accountability

Public institutions must answer to the people they serve. We will establish independent oversight with real enforcement power — not frameworks designed to absorb complaints without acting on them.

Environmental Justice

The Verdoyante disaster was not an accident of fate. It was the result of ignored warnings and inadequate regulation. We will rebuild the inspection frameworks that failed Pleucadeuc.

Real Security

Security is not a slogan. The ManMan Centre attack exposed a chain of command that collapsed under pressure. Social Democratic Alliance will demand a full independent inquiry and structural reform.

Workers' Dignity

A worker who files a safety complaint should never wonder whether it will disappear. Mandatory response timelines, enforceable standards, and the right to organise without fear.

Regional Voice

Republicanism without regional representation is just centralism with better rhetoric. Every constituency deserves a representative who is present, not one who arrives only at election time.

Civic Foundation

The republic is not a word. It is a promise — that the institutions of this country were built for the people inside it. We intend to keep that promise.

Two crises. One government. No answers.

The Gallen spill. The ManMan Centre attack. In both cases, warnings were ignored, systems failed, and people paid the price. Avenir Caprica is asking you to reward that record. We are asking you to change it.
