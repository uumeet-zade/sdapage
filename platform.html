Our Platform

The Social Democratic Alliance is committed to the view that the dignity of each individual person is the most essential principle in any democratic society. We unite people of different faiths and beliefs who are committed to peace, freedom, justice, and solidarity.

The 5 Core Principles

The SDA is dedicated to the principles that have built our party: Solidarity, Liberty, Tolerance, Social Justice, and Equality. We are fighting to establish a framework of meaningful participation by all persons in a mass-based participatory democracy.

[ Image Placeholder: Unity & Solidarity ]
A Multi-Ethnic Caprica

We defend our unique and diverse multi-ethnic community. While recognizing Alanian as our lingua franca, we fiercely protect Gallic, Alcamerian, Ralandic, and indigenous languages as essential pieces of culture that shape the Caprican nation in a global society.

[ Image Placeholder: Diversity & Culture ]
The Welfare State & The Working Masses

We reaffirm the expansion of the welfare state. Healthcare, education, housing, and social security are fundamental entitlements, not privileges. We fight to secure the full fruits of collective effort for the working class upon the basis of common ownership and progressive reform.

[ Image Placeholder: Working Masses & Housing ]
International Solidarity

We stand with the international democratic socialist movement. As an affiliate member of the Forward Parliamentary Group in the Columbian Union Parliament, we advocate for a rules-based international order founded upon peace, mutual respect, and national self-determination.

[ Image Placeholder: Columbian Union & Peace ]

The Complete Constitution

Our vision extends far beyond these four pillars. The SDA Constitution comprehensively addresses our internal structures, including our powerful grassroots Branches, the Social Democratic Youth wing, the SDAqueer LGBTQIA+ wing, and our close, formal cooperation with affiliated labour unions.
