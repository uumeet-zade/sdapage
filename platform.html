Our Platform

The Republic of Virtue is not an inheritance. It is a choice—one that must be made anew by every generation. We stand for the Third Republic. We stand for Caprica.

The Third Republic & Transparency

We will introduce an unassailable separation of powers and strict anti-corruption legislation. No more closed doors. No more redacted reports. The Caprican state belongs to its citizens, not to oligarchs or foreign lobbies.

[ Image Placeholder: Constitutional Reform ]
The Caprican School of Meritocratic Development

A republic within a republic. We will establish self-contained residential academic campuses for Caprica's brightest students, regardless of their family's wealth. The state provides everything—from housing to a performance-linked allowance—ensuring talent finds its destination.

[ Image Placeholder: Education / CSMD ]
Housing, Rail & Infrastructure

Every Caprican deserves a home. We will liberalise building regulations to dramatically increase supply, while implementing a severe, progressive multi-property tax on speculators holding three or more homes. Furthermore, a high-speed rail network will connect every state capital.

[ Image Placeholder: High-Speed Rail & Housing ]
Universal Healthcare & Unapologetic Science

Healthcare is a right, not a market. We will expand the public system and force down pharmaceutical prices. Furthermore, we commit to massive R&D investments and the expansion of nuclear energy as the rational, clean choice for our industrial future.

[ Image Placeholder: Science & Nuclear Energy ]

The Complete Manifesto

Our vision extends far beyond these four pillars. The SDA manifesto comprehensively addresses Foreign Policy (solidifying our Columbian Union membership and military oversight), Social Equality (uncompromising LGBTQ+ protections, gender equality, and compassionate drug rehabilitation), and Digital Rights (breaking up media monopolies and combating disinformation).
